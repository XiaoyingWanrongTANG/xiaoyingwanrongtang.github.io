General

Dr. Tang is a postdoctoral scholar at EPFL, working with Prof. Jean-Yves Le Boudec and Prof. Mario Paolone. She received her Ph.D. from the department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2016, supervised by Prof. Angela Yingjun Zhang. Dr. Tang's previous name is Wanrong Tang.

Research Interests

Online algorithm, scheduling algorithm and distributed algorithm design and optimization in network systems, e.g., smart grid and intelligent transportation network Fundamental research in optimization theory, machine learning and theoretical computer science
