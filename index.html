General

Dr. Tang is a postdoctoral scholar at EPFL, working with Prof. Jean-Yves Le Boudec and Prof. Mario Paolone. She received her Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2016, supervised by Prof. Angela Yingjun Zhang. Dr. Tang's previous name is Wanrong Tang.

Research Interests

Online scheduling algorithm; Disributed algorithm; Optimization; Smart grid.
